Read fluently and comfortably, with simple and clear interface.

Supported Document Types:
PDF, EPUB, MOBI, CBZ, FB2, XPS, TXT, HTML, OFFICE(DOCX, XLSX, PPTX) and ZIP/GZIP

Features:
· Horizontal / Vertical Flip
· Continuous Scroll
· Lock Stray: Keep scroll straight
· Margin Crop
· Focus Mode: Keep page position on flipping zoomed pages
· Smarty Focus: Recognize page border bias in Focus Mode
· RtL Text: Support Top-to-Bottom, Right-to-Left(TB-RL) text
· Single Column: Support scanned dual-spread PDF page
· Color Palette
· Font Size selectable
· Text Copy
· Table of Contents
· Bookmarks
· Links
· Search
· Share a book
· Remember reading status

The project is under active development.
